Although most people in Japan use, or at least are familiar with, the Western calendar (seireki), the traditional calendar (wareki) is still used for official documents such as a resume and driver's license.

The traditional Japanese calendar is based on the reign period of the emperor. Each time a different emperor begins to rule, a new counting of the years begins and the period acquires a new name.
